About Me

Hi! I'm a PhD student at UC Berkeley advised by Alane Suhr, affiliated with Berkeley AI Research and Berkeley NLP. I received my B.S. in Computer Science from the University of Colorado Boulder in May 2024 under Katharina von der Wense in the NALA lab. My research interests broadly include natural language processing and machine learning. More specifically, I have worked on NLP for education and low-resource langauge revitalization. I'm currently interested in cognitively inspired and multimodal approaches to NLP.

I grew up in Boulder, Colorado and enjoy hiking, reading, movies, piano, and travel. Currently learning Spanish, Japanese, and Korean.
