About Me

Hi! My name is Téa and I am a Computer Science undergraduate student at University of Colorado, Boulder. My research interests include natural language processing, psycholinguistics, and endangered language revitalization.

I grew up in Boulder and love hiking, reading, watching movies, and playing piano. I also love to travel and learn new languages!
