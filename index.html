About Me

Hi! I'm a PhD student at UC Berkeley advised by Alane Suhr affiliated with Berkeley AI Research and Berkeley NLP. I recieved my B.S. in Computer Science from the University of Colorado Boulder in May 2024 where I was advised by Katharina von der Wense in the NALA lab. My research interests broadly include natural language processing and machine learning. More specifically, I have worked on NLP for education and low-resource langauge revitalization. I'm currently interested in cognitively inspired NLP approaches and computational psycholinguistics.

I grew up in Boulder, Colorado and love hiking, reading, watching movies, and playing piano. I also love to travel and learn new languages! I'm currently learning Spanish, Japanese, and Korean :)
